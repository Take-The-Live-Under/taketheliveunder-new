Print this out. Check off daily. Consistency wins.

## Emergency Content Ideas

When you don't know what to post:

**Reddit:**
- "Interesting stat I found about [top 10 team]"
- "Underrated teams according to the numbers"
- "Which conference is actually the best?"
- Answer someone's question with data

**Twitter:**
- Poll: "Who wins tonight?"
- Hot take with stats to back it up
- Respond to a big account's take with data
- "One stat that will surprise you about [team]"

**TikTok:**
- Screen record interesting matchup comparison
- React to someone else's bad take
- "POV: You're checking if your team is good"
- Quick tutorial of the research tool
